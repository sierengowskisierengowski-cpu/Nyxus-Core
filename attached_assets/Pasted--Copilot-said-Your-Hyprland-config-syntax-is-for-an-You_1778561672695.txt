Likely syntax conversions
These are the common valid modern forms.

Window rules
Usually:

INI
windowrulev2 = float, class:^(io\.nyxus\.weather)$
windowrulev2 = center, class:^(io\.nyxus\.weather)$
windowrulev2 = pin, class:^(panel|nyxus-panel)$
windowrulev2 = noborder, class:^(io\.nyxus\.sysmon)$
windowrulev2 = opacity 0.95 0.95, class:^(io\.nyxus\.weather)$
Not something malformed like:

INI
windowrulev2 float, center, class:^(io\.nyxus\.weather)$
or:

INI
windowrulev2 = class:^(io\.nyxus\.weather)$, float
if your version doesn’t accept that order.

Layer rules
Usually:

INI
layerrule = blur, waybar
layerrule = ignorealpha 0.2, waybar
layerrule = noanim, launcher
layerrule = noblur, notifications
If Hyprland says invalid field blur or type ignorealpha, it often means the line is not being parsed as a proper layerrule = ... assignment.
