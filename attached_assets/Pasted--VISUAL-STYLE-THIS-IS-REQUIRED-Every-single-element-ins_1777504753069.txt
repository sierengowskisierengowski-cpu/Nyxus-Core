
VISUAL STYLE — THIS IS REQUIRED:
Every single element inside and outside the app must have a 
hand drawn hand written feel throughout. This means:
- All buttons look hand drawn — slightly imperfect borders, 
  sketch-like outlines, not perfect rectangles
- All panels and containers have hand drawn borders — 
  like someone drew them with a marker
- All fonts are handwritten style throughout — titles, 
  labels, buttons, everything
- Dividers and separators look hand drawn — like a pen line
- Icons are hand drawn sketch style — not Material icons
- Input fields look like lined notebook paper or sketch pads
- The entire app feels like a physical notebook or sketchpad 
  that has been brought to life digitally
- Scrollbars styled to look hand drawn
- Tab indicators look like sticky tab labels
- Nothing looks like a standard GTK app — every widget 
  must be custom drawn to match the hand drawn aesthetic
- Use Cairo custom drawing for all non-standard elements
